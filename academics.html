Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive curriculum recognized by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

All major streams including Science, Commerce, and Humanities are available.

Our Curriculum

We follow the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) syllabus with modern teaching methods and student-centric learning.

Playway Level

Focus on play-based learning and early childhood development.

Primary School

Grades 1 to 5: Foundational skills in language, math, science, and social studies.

Middle School

Grades 6 to 8: Subject-specific learning begins with enhanced focus on critical thinking.

Secondary School

Grades 9 to 10: Preparing students for board exams with full support and guidance.

Senior Secondary

Class 11–12: Choose from Science, Commerce, or Humanities streams.

Co-Curricular

Art, music, sports, robotics, coding, and life skills integrated into the daily routine.

Available Streams

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Biology / Mathematics, Computer Science, English

Commerce Stream

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Math / Informatics Practices, English

Humanities Stream

History, Geography, Political Science, Psychology, English
